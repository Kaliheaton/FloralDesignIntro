Student Florist

Wherever you may be, there are resources available for you to start your own experience with floral design. There are countless youtube channels, podcasts, and free websites where florists share their technical skills and experiences. There are video tutorials, walkthroughs, and other linked resources that will help you.

There are also in-person communities you can join! Local flower shops can help provide guidance. Social media pages can also help direct you to other people in your area with a shared interest. Once you've gotten connected, you can involve other people you may know and help the community grow even stronger.
