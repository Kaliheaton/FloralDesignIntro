Floral Design

A Guide for Beginners

Where to Start

No matter who you are or what phase of life you may find yourself in, floral design could be the path for you! Think about why you may be drawn to floral design, and then pursue it! Reseach what local resources may be available, and get involved!

My experience

My journey with flowers started this year, when I got a job at a local flower shop. Prior to this, I had no interest in flowers or plants, mostly because I never seemed to be able to keep them alive myself. However, as I started this job, I began to learn more about plant care, and grew to appreciate the beauty that I have always been surrounded with. Now, flowers are a great source of joy for me.

Benefits

Learning and practicing more in floral design has changed the way that I perceive the world. Now, I am more confident in my ability to develop new skills and interests. My eyes have also been opened to the natural beauty of the world around me. Wherever I go, I look more closely at my surroundings, and it makes me so happy when I find a flower or plant that I hadn't seen before.
